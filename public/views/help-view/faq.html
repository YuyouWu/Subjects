Frequently Asked Questions

We are currently aggregating more frequently asked questions. If you have anymore questions, you can reach us at syllabus_support@gmail.com.

  1. What is syllabus?

    Syllabus is a site that aggregates online learning resources for any subjects. We let users rate those resources in order to create a study guide for these subjects. Our goal is to create a crowdsourced study guide and accelerate the learning process.

  2. What do I do If I don't see the subject I want to learn?

    You can register an account and request a new subject to be added. After you've registered, click on your username on the top right corner, then click on "Request Subject" and enter the subject you want to add.

  3. How do I change my password?

    Click on your username, and then click on settings. There you have a link to change your password.
